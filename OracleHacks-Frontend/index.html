FAQ

What is the Contracts & Chargebacks Solution?

The Contracts & Chargebacks solution eliminates chargeback errors, by automatically settling chargeback transactions against aligned pricing contracts and established business rules.
Through the solution pharmaceutical and other healthcare product manufacturers, wholesalers and GPOs get a unified view of:

    Clean/validated GPO membership rosters
    Customer Identifier data (HIN, DEA, 340B)
    Contract headers, pricing, customers

Why Should pharmaceutical manufacturers join the network?

For pharmaceutical manufacturers provides a platform for industry level innovation and automation with your trading partners. Value comes in many forms whether an organization adopts an existing commercial solution or joins a working group to help shape new protocol and future industry solutions from the ground up.

The Product Verification solution delivers cost effective compliance with DSCSA, as well as improved industry collaboration around product transfers of ownership.

Other benefits include the elimination of unnecessary add/re-bills for improved customer service, more accurate/timely government pricing based on accurate chargeback data, reduced compliance risk from real-time adherence to the DEA database, immediate contract activation and (for generic manufacturers) increased sales from faster price changes in wholesaler systems.

Why Should healthcare product manufacturers join the network?

The Network has expanded the Contracts & Chargebacks solution from the pharmaceutical industry into the medical, surgical and healthcare product industry.

The Contracts & Chargebacks Solution delivers real value to both the top and bottom line for healthcare product manufacturers. Manufacturers who join the network with Contracts & Chargebacks see massive time savings by eliminating/improving manual processes like resolving chargeback errors, evaluating new customers, managing GPO membership and auditing customer master data.

Manufacturers also see a large reduction in revenue leakage from the elimination of unapproved deductions/unresolved chargeback settlements, incorrect chargeback approvals, credits/returns incorrectly rejected as duplicates and low dollar chargebacks under the threshold for review.

Why Should distributors join the network?

For Distributors in the healthcare industry, efficiency and automation are crucial. The Network offers a completely new category of automation with trading partners, by enforcing rules between companies in the same way internal systems are integrated.

The Product Verification solution delivers cost effective compliance with DSCSA, as well as improved industry collaboration around product transfers of ownership. Through blockchain technology, and quality execution the Network offers some of the fastest response times in the industry on product verification.
